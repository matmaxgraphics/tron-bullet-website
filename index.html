Frequently Asked Questions

TronBullet is a sniping bot designed for the TRON network that allows you to execute token trades with exceptional speed and precision. Operated through Telegram, TronBullet makes it easy for users to buy TRON-based tokens at their desired prices, ensuring they can capitalize on market opportunities as quickly as possible.

Getting started with TronBullet is simple:

  • Download Telegram if you haven’t already.
  • Search for the TronBullet bot on Telegram and start it.
  • Fund your wallet with TRX tokens to begin trading.
  • Paste the contract address (CA) of the token you want to buy, and let TronBullet handle the rest.

When you launch TronBullet on Telegram, the bot will automatically generate a unique TRON wallet address for you. This address is used to deposit TRX, which will then be used to execute trades on the platform.

To deposit TRX into your TronBullet wallet:

  1. Copy the wallet address provided by the bot.
  2. Transfer TRX from any external wallet or exchange to this address.
  3. The bot will monitor the blockchain and update your balance as soon as the transaction is confirmed.

Withdrawing your funds from TronBullet is straightforward:

  1. Sell your tokens back into TRX using the bot.
  2. Transfer the TRX from your TronBullet wallet to any external wallet of your choice.
  3. The withdrawal process is quick, and the funds should reflect in your external wallet shortly after the transaction is confirmed on the TRON network

TronBullet prioritizes security at every level. The bot processes all transactions securely via the TRON blockchain, ensuring your funds are safe. Additionally, TronBullet uses encryption to protect your data and does not store sensitive information like private keys. You retain full control over your assets at all times.

TronBullet charges a flat trading fee of 0.5% on each transaction. This fee is deducted automatically when you execute a trade, making it simple and transparent with no hidden costs. Additionally, you can earn a 30% bonus on fees generated by users you refer to the platform.
